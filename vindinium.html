Vindinium!

Vindinium is, in essence, an online game where 4 players fight against each other to become the king of Vindinium. This is done through the claiming of mines and the retrieval of gold. You must fight other players in order to gain control of their mines. You lose health whenever you move, fight another player, and steal another player's mine. You regain health through the buying of drinks from a tavern, which is where the gold comes in. You obtain gold from the mines you collect, making this a continuous cycle. There is a limit to the game though, since there are only a certain number of turns and when that time is up, whoever has the most mines at the end becomes the ruler of Vindinium. You play by programming your players, or your bots, to be able to attack or go to the tavern. This would result in your bot healing themself or defeating another bot. Both are helpful, but by setting certain if statements, you will be able to control the movements of your bot. By doing this, you will be able to be even better if you know the code.
